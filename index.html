Your New Favourite Cocktail App

Iced Out is a cocktail making app with the goal of helping adults who like to drink cocktails find the perfect drink no matter the occasion. Whether it be for relaxing after a long day after work or hosting a small get-together, cocktail enjoyers can discover and make their new favourite recipes. Through our preference-based suggestions, user reviews, and refined search filters we will enable cocktail drinkers to make informed decisions about new cocktail recipes to enjoy or prepare for their guests. Let's have a drink!
